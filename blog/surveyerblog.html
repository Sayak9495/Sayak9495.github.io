Internet Surveyer

I was in standard 8th. And I don’t know why but suddenly felt like coding. Maybe it was Die Hard 4, maybe the documentaries I’ve been watching, maybe something else, but I needed to code. So, I was just learning some C# on MSDN(channel 9) and suddenly I had some idea to make a simple web-browser. I figured out a simple layout, noted down the features I wanted. Made the simple on my dad’s office-PC. Then I used to go to YouTube and learned how to implement the codes for the specific feature, sometimes it would take days. I kept on repeating the process, until all the features were strike out. And surprisingly it took only a month until the final project was rolled out. So that’s how, I completed my first real project.
